Welcome to Battleship Matrix

The board is 30 spaces wide and 30 spaces high, made on a multidimensional array. All coordinates have a 0 in them, except for the ones that have a ship which have a number 1.

The ships that exist in this board are: Carrier, Battleship, Cruiser, Submarine and Destroyer.

Ask for a position on the board and the console will answer wether you sinked a ship or not. If you hit a position that belongs to a ship you sink the entire ship.
