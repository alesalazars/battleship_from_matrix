Welcome to Battleship Matrix

The board is 20 spaces wide and 20 spaces high, made on a multidimensional array. All coordinates have a 0 in them, except for the ones that have a ship which have a number 1.

Click on the board and the console will answer wether you sinked a ship or not. If you hit a position that belongs to a ship you sink the entire ship.

The ships that exist in this board are:

Carrier
Battleship
Cruiser
Submarine
Destroyer
